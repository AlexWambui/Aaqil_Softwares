Spanish Omelette

Ingredients:

  1. 1 large red onion.
  2. 1 table spoon of cooking oil.
  3. 2 large potatoes.
  4. 2 chicken franks/ sausages / smokies.
  5. 4 eggs.
  6. 30g cheese.

Steps:

  1. Fry the onions.
  2. chop the potatoes into slices then small pieces, like cubes.
  3. Add the potatoes to the onions and cook for 3 - 4 minutes under medium heat while occasionally stirring.
  4. Add the chopped chicken franks to potatoes.
  5. Cover and fry for 8 - 10 minutes then flip to cook the other side while covered.

Porridge

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup of water.
  2. 2 table spoons of porridge flour.
  3. 4 table spoons of Sugar.
  4. 1 table spoon of blue band.
  5. 1/2 cup of milk.

Steps:

  1. Bring the water to boil.
  2. In a bowl, mix half a cup of cold water and porridge flour.
  3. pour into the boiling water as you continually stir to avoid lumps.
  4. Once mixed and boiling, add blueband and then stir.
  5. Let it boild for 2 - 3 minutes.
  6. Add sugar and let it boil for a minute.
  7. Add milk and let it boil for a minute as you stir.
  8. Serve while hot.
