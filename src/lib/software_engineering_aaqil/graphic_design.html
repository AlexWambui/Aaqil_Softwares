Introduction

Colors

Purple

Use purple in your brand to make it look: luxurious, expensive, mysterious, respectable, wealthy, creative, imaginative and rare.

Purple influences emotions such as: excitement, mystery and prestige.

Logo Design

Logo Design Process

When designing a logo, try to answer the three question:

  1. What does the brand sell?
  2. Who do they sell to? [age, gender, spending power, race and online interaction].
  3. What emotions does the brand intend to communicate?
